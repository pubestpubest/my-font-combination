What is Typography?

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed.

Roboto

Roboto is a neo-grotesque sans-serif typeface developed by Google. It's the default font for Android and Chrome OS.

Times New Roman

Times New Roman is a serif typeface designed for legibility in body text. It was commissioned by the British newspaper The Times in 1931.

JetBrains Mono

JetBrains Mono is a monospace font specifically designed for developers. It includes coding ligatures and enhanced readability features.
